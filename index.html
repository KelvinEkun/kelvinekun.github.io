Research

My current research focus is on online learning and decision-making under uncertainty, with applications to make-to-order manufacturing and service firms.

I am also interested in graph neural networks, neural algorithmic reasoning, and primal-dual algorithms, which form the methodological backbone of my work.

Previously, I was a research associate Reeddi, where I worked on predicting emissions from supply chain equipment. I also have professional experience in the fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) industry as a graduate intern at Heneiken subsidiary (Nigerian Breweries) as well as an undergraduate intern at British American Tobacco (BAT). In both experiences, I worked on continuous improvement and total productivity management.
