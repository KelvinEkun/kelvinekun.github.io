Hello, I’m Kelvin Ekun. I’m an Industrial Engineering PhD candidate at UMass interested in the intersection of supply chain management and machine learning.

Research

My current focus is on online learning and decision-making under uncertainty, with applications to make-to-order manufacturing and service firms.

I am also interested in graph neural networks, primal-dual algorithms, and neural algorithmic reasoning, which form the methodological backbone of my work.

Previously, I was a Research Associate working on applied optimization and learning-based decision systems.
